Kerala Trip

10-Day Kerala Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kochi

Explore Fort Kochi, visit Chinese fishing nets, and enjoy local cuisine.

Day 2: Munnar

Visit tea plantations, Eravikulam National Park, and enjoy hill station views.

Day 3: Alleppey Backwaters

Houseboat ride through serene backwaters, enjoy Kerala cuisine onboard.

Day 4: Thekkady

Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary, bamboo rafting, spice plantations visit.

Day 5: Kumarakom

Relax by the backwaters, bird sanctuary visit, and sunset cruise.

Day 6: Kovalam

Relax at beaches, lighthouse visit, enjoy Kerala spa therapy.

Day 7: Varkala

Cliffside beach, yoga sessions, and sunset watching at Papanasam beach.

Day 8: Houseboat Experience

Another day in Kerala backwaters to relax and enjoy local cuisine.

Day 9: Cultural Tour

Attend Kathakali performance, visit local temples, and markets.

Day 10: Departure

Return from Kochi airport, farewell Kerala!
