North Goa Day Trip

Morning: Visit Fort Aguada and SinQ Beach.

Late Morning: Relax at Baga Beach or Calangute Beach; try water sports.

Lunch: Beach shack at Baga or Calangute (Goan seafood).

Afternoon: Explore Anjuna Beach & Flea Market.

Evening: Sunset at Chapora Fort. Optional nightlife at Tito's Lane or Club Cubana.

South Goa Day Trip

Morning: Visit Palolem Beach; optional dolphin spotting or kayaking.

Late Morning: Explore Cabo De Rama Fort.

Lunch: Beach shack at Agonda or Palolem Beach (fresh seafood).

Afternoon: Relax at Colva Beach or Butterfly Beach; optional spice plantation visit.

Evening: Sunset at Palolem or Colva Beach; return to hotel.
